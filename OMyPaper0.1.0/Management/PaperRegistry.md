# Paper Registry

One row per paper. The registry is keyed by `citekey` and acts as the management-side index that connects raw notes, formal paper wiki pages, and current status.

## Status Legend

- `raw-only`: raw note exists, no formal wiki page yet
- `ingested`: paper wiki exists
- `needs-review`: page exists but needs manual review
- `unmatched`: metadata conflict or unstable identity
