# Paper Registry

- project membership is **not** recorded here

## Status Legend

- `raw-only`: raw note exists but no durable paper wiki yet
- `ingested`: paper wiki exists in `Notes/Papers/`
- `needs-review`: identity or content exists but still needs review
- `unmatched`: paper identity or file linkage is not trustworthy yet
